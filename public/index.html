I just received my IELTS results, and I scored a band 7.0 listening 6.5, reading, 7.0, writing, 7.0, and speaking 7.5. Can I study in the UK with this?

by Kamol Zaripov on May 14, 2022

Well, as an English qualification it would be fine, no problem at all. Most UK universities want a minimum of 6.5 overall, which is roughly equivalent to a decent GCSE English grade, so 7.0 is fine. However, that is just the basic language qualification. Nobody in the UK goes to university just because they've managed to pass GCSE English.
